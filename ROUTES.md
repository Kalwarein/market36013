# Market360 Routes Documentation

## Main Navigation (Bottom Tabs)
- `/` - Home page with quick actions and product carousels
- `/categories` - Category listing page
- `/messenger` - Messenger inbox with conversations list
- `/cart` - Shopping cart
- `/profile` - User profile and account management

## Product & Shopping
- `/product/:id` - Product detail page
- `/search` - Search results with filters
- `/listings/:category` - Category-specific product listings
- `/ranking` - Top ranking products (daily/weekly/monthly)
- `/checkout` - Checkout page

## User Account
- `/settings` - Main settings page
- `/settings/account` - Account information & profile photo
- `/settings/notifications` - Push & email notification preferences
- `/settings/language` - Language selection
- `/settings/security` - Password, 2FA, sessions
- `/settings/about` - App info, terms, contact

## Orders & Favorites
- `/orders` - Order history with status tabs
- `/orders/:orderId` - Order detail with tracking
- `/favorites` - Saved/favorited products
- `/history` - Recently viewed products
- `/coupons` - Available coupons and credits

## Help & Support
- `/help` - Help center main page
- `/help/:topic` - Help topic articles (payment, shipping, returns, protection)
- `/support/chat` - Live chat with support team

## Business Features
- `/quotation` - Request for quotation form
- `/customization` - Fast customization services
- `/conversation/:conversationId` - Individual seller conversation

## Seller Routes
- `/seller/setup` - Seller onboarding
- `/seller/dashboard` - Seller dashboard with stats
- `/seller/store` - Manage store (redirects to dashboard)
- `/seller/products` - Product management
- `/seller/products/new` - Add new product
- `/seller/orders` - Seller order management
- `/seller/earnings` - Earnings & transactions
- `/seller/analytics` - Analytics (placeholder)
